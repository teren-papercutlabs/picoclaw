# Serene Glow — Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you require a deposit to book?**
A: A 50% deposit is required for packages (Bridal, Serene Retreat, Express Glow) and for any service over AED 400. Single services under AED 400 can be booked without a deposit. Deposits are refundable with 24 hours notice.

**Q: What is your cancellation policy?**
A: We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may forfeit the deposit. For packages, we request 48 hours notice.

**Q: Do you offer services for men?**
A: Yes, all our services are available to everyone — haircuts, massages, facials, nail care, and waxing. We welcome all guests.

**Q: Are your products cruelty-free?**
A: We use professional-grade products that are cruelty-free across all treatments. Many of our retail products are also vegan-certified. Please ask our team for specific product details during your visit.

**Q: Can children receive services?**
A: Our salon is designed for guests aged 16 and above. Guests between 16–17 are welcome with parental consent.

**Q: Can I request a specific therapist or stylist?**
A: Yes, you may request a preferred therapist or stylist when booking. Requests are subject to availability. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred team member.

**Q: Are your treatment rooms private?**
A: Yes. All massage, facial, and body treatment rooms are fully private with individual changing facilities. Couples massage takes place in a dedicated couples suite.

**Q: Do you sell gift vouchers?**
A: Yes. Gift vouchers are available in-salon and can be arranged via WhatsApp. Denominations: AED 200, AED 500, AED 1,000, or any custom amount. Valid for 12 months from purchase.

**Q: Do you offer home visit services?**
A: We do not currently offer home visits. All services are provided at our Dubai Marina salon.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, and Apple Pay. Payment is taken at the end of your visit (or deposit at booking for packages).
