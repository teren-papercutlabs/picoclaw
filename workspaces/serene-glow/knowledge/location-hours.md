# Serene Glow — Location & Hours

## Address

**Unit 204, Marina Walk Tower**
Dubai Marina, Dubai, UAE

Near Dubai Marina Mall (2-minute walk). Located on the second floor of Marina Walk Tower, overlooking the marina promenade.

## Getting Here

**By Metro:**
Dubai Marina Station (Red Line) — approximately 10-minute walk along the waterfront.
Alternatively, DMCC Metro Station is a 12-minute walk.

**By Car / Taxi:**
Address to give your driver: Marina Walk Tower, Dubai Marina.
Taxis can drop off at the Marina Walk entrance.

**Parking:**
Marina Walk outdoor parking — first hour complimentary, AED 5 per hour thereafter.
Valet parking available at Marina Walk (AED 20 flat fee).

**By Bus:**
Routes 8, 84, and X28 stop at Dubai Marina Mall, a 2-minute walk away.
